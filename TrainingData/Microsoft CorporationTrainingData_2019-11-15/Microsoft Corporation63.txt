Solange and her second husband, Alan Ferguson, have separated, she confirmed on Friday.The 33-year-old singer shared a lengthy Instagram post about her "physical and spiritual transition" that revealed she and the music video director "parted ways" earlier this year. A post shared by @ saintrecords on Nov 1, 2019 at 12:18pm PDTNov 1, 2019 at 12:18pm PDT
"ive lived my best and worst moments in front of the lens and gaze of the world since i was a teenager. ive always tried to live in my truth no matter how ugly or full of love it is," she wrote.Solange went on to add, "11 years ago i met a phenomenal man who changed every existence of my life. early this year we separated and parted ways, (and tho it ain't nan no body business) i find it necessary to protect the sacredness of my personal truth and to live in it fully just as I have before and will continue to do."Solange tied the knot with Ferguson in November 2014 at New Orleans' Marigny Opera House. The ceremony was attended by friends and family, including Solange's sister Beyoncé, brother-in-law Jay-Z, and niece Blue Ivy. Solange and Ferguson don't share any children together, although the songwriter has a 15-year-old son whom she shares with ex-husband Daniel Smith. Like what you see here?
